Strong Password Guidelines

General Password Guidelines

  • Use a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Password should be a minimum of 12 charachters.

Memorable Passwords

  • Use 3 Simple Words.
  • Password should be between 12 to 20 charachters.

Password Best Practice

  • Do not share your passwords with anyone.
  • Never write down your password.
  • Do not include personal information in your password. For example, pet names or special dates.
  • Do not use the same password for multiple accounts.

  • Consider the use of a Password Manager.
  • Consider setting MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication) to your accounts.
